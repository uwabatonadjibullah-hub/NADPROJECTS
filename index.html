A Journey of Complex Agony & Healing Peace

In 1978, Louise, the daughter of a rigid Belgian diplomat, and Jack, a Rwandan returnee from Uganda, found home in each other within the walls of the Hôtel des Mille Collines.

What followed was a decades-long odyssey of political betrayal, unspeakable loss, and a secret legacy that would only be revealed through the pages of a final memoir: Nakunze Urudashoboka.
